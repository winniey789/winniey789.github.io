Instructions

1. Open GarageBand

Select “Empty Project”

Choose “Software Instrument” when prompted

2. Set Time Signature

At the top, change the time signature to 3/4

3. Open Loop Browser

Click the loop icon (looks like a loop or rope) in the top right

4. Browse Loops

Search or filter by instrument (e.g., “drums” or “percussion”)

Click to preview

5. Drag a Loop to the Track Area

Choose a loop that works rhythmically in 3/4 (some loops are flexible)

Drag it into the workspace

6. Trim to 1 Bar

If the loop is longer than 3 beats, trim it to one 3/4 bar by adjusting the region's end

7. Loop It

Hover over the top-right edge of the looped region

When the loop icon appears, drag to repeat the 3-beat loop

8. Add More Loops (Optional)

Add basslines, synths, or FX — just be sure they fit the 3/4 rhythm

9. Save or Export

Go to Share > Export Song to Disk
