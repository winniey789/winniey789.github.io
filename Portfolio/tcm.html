Living Archive, 2025


Exhibiton: People, Places, Things at the Addams Hall Fine Arts Gallery

Skills: Curation, Exhibition design, Lighting design, Collaborative event production

Overview: Cataloging the interior of a Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) pharmacy, Living Archive traces the labor and cultural memory held in ordinary tools. The charcoal drawings and recipe book honor a healing practice carried through objects as well as people.
