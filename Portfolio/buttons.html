Buttons, 2025


Skills: Ceramics, Sewing, Exhibition design, Lighting design

Overview: This project was completed for my design senior seminar class. Buttons reimagines the dress shirt as a site of material exploration, specifically clay. I was inspired by Hella Jongerius’s works, intersecting craftsmanship and industrial production. So, I handcrafted clay buttons to replace the standardized plastic ones on a dress shirt. It introduces variation, individuality, and a dialogue between function and ornament within a traditionally uniform garment.
