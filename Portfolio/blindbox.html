Timeless Tales, 2024


Skills: Photoshop, Illustrator, 3D-printing, AI (ChatGPT & Mid-Journey)

Overview: Timeless Tales is a blind box design exploring memory and keepsakes. The packaging uses nostalgic colors to evoke a sense of reminiscence, while each blind box contains one of three 3D-printed items: a picture frame, luggage, or memory box, representing different forms of memory. Each item contains an embedded NFT chip, allowing collectors to attach any digital file (image, video, or other media) transforming the physical object into a vessel for their most cherished digital memories.
