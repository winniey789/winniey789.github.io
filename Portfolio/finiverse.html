Finiverse

Affiliation: The University of Pennsylvania
Timeline: August 2022 - present
Role: Product Design & Development Team
Tools: Figma, Illustrator, InDesign

Finiverse is a college and career planning tool designed to help students make informed decisions about financing their education. I worked on product design, UX flows, and interaction design across multiple modules, including the onboarding simulation tutorial and branded module graphics.

Overview

Finiverse is an educational financial planning platform that helps students understand college financing, student loans, repayment plans, and career outcomes. Our goal was to simplify complex financial concepts through clear UI, structured modules, and an interactive risk simulation tutorial.

My Contributions

  • Designed the onboarding simulation tutorial and risk walkthrough
  • Created module graphics and visual identity system
  • Developed UX improvements to navigation and information architecture
  • Produced printable handouts for in-person workshops

Impact

The final experience improved student comprehension and reduced confusion around loan terminology. Workshop instructors reported clearer student understanding, and the Finiverse design system is now used across all module updates.
