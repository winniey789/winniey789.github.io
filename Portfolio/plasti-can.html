Plasti-Can, 2022


Skills: Blender, Illustrator

Overview: This project explores speculative product design through the creation of a fictional, sci-fi–inspired trash can. I designed a trash can that instantly converts plastic waste into reusable material with the push of a button, reframing disposal as an active, regenerative process rather than a passive one. I also developed a system of interface icons (i.e power, settings, and operation states) to communicate how users would interact with the device.
